About us

Alba Wildlife Cruises is based in the tiny port of Morar on the western coast of Scotland. We operate a passenger ferry which holds up to 30 passengers. It is also available to carry provisions and mail to the three islands which it supports. These islands are Eigg, Rum and Muck.
